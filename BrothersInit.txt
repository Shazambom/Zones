How to define a brother: Just give them a name and email address
Everything below the brothers list line will be seen as a brother until there is no more lines
Example:Ian,mrderp14@gmail.com
There must be no lines including empty lines below brother list that are not Brother object definitions otherwise
errors will be thrown.

You can define the threshold before hand by typing all capital threshold =x (x as in the number you want)
You can also define the error before hand by typing all capital error =y (y as in the number you want)
